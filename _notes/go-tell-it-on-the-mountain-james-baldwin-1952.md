[[BookNotes]] author: [[James-Baldwin]]

Novel dealing with one black family and a church sevirce, based in 1930's Harlem. 
- Hypnotic sermon like writing, incredible on a sentence level. Like Virginia Woolf in how it moves from one perspective to another seamlessly. All the characters feel so alive.
- Based around one night at a church and one conversion87.
- Everyone is flawed and sinners, everyone are hypocrites but the religious experience is never dismissed.
- Ever present threat of racist violence in the background. Community comes together against the threats.
- Brilliant. Want to read more.
