This is an idea for [[Journaling]] from Anne Marie LeCuff. Make three columns with a plus, a minus and an arrow. Quickly write down what went well, what didn't and what's next. 

It's a useful way to conduct a quick [[Weekly-Review]] and embrace the fact that [[journaling-allows-you-to-be-present]].
