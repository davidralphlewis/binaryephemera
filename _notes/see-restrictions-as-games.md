Of course you know that [[Restrictions-Make-You-More-Creative]], but it can be difficult to allow yourself to dive into restrictions. One way to reframe this is to see restrictions as rules in a game. You aren't expecting a result, instead you are playing a game and seeing what happens. 

## References

- [[Ross-Sutherland]] often describes his process this way.
- This was evident in [[this-is-what-you-get-stanley-donwood-and-thom-yorke-exhibition]], with a challenge not to use the undo function on a computer. This resulted in the art for *OK Computer*
