author [[Taiyo Matsumoto]]

A manga, a genre I haven't had a lot of experience in. I enjoyed this though. A manga editor leaves the business  burnt out from the commercial pressure. Art is hard to resist though  and soon he is putting together a team to write a new series. Slow and reflective, this features stunning drawings of the city and precisely observed characters.
