[[film-notes]]

- Beautiful animation based on the illustrated manuscript. Celtic inspired design, stylised animation with lots of swirls and patterns. Find it much more expressive than photo-realism.
- BAsed on a lot of fact - Kells did hold the manuscript after Iona was destroyed by viking raids.
- Story is a mix of the factual and mythological.
